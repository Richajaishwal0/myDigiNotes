## Features of DES
- Symmetric Block Cipher
- Replaced by AES in early 2000s
- Input: 64 bits
- Output: 64 bits
- Main key: 64 bits
- Sub Key: 56 bits
- Round Key: 48 bits
- No. of rounds = 16 rounds
