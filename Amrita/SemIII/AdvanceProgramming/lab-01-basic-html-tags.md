## AIM: To create an HTML document that demonstrates various inline CSS styles. 

### Code

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
<title>HTML Styles Example</title>
</head>
<body>
<h4>5. HTML Styles</h4>
<p>I am normal</p>
<p style="color:red;">I am red</p>
<p style="color: blue;">I am blue</p>
<p style="font-size:50px;">I am big</p>
<p style="font-style: italic;">I am italic</p>
<p style="font-weight:bold;">I am bold</p>
<p style="text-decoration:underline;">I am underlined</p>
<p style="background-color: yellow;">I have a yellow background</p>
<p style="border:2px solid black;">I have a border</p>
<p style="text-align:center; ">I am centered</p>
</body>
</html>
```
